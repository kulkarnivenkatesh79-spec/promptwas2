Step by Step

The Election Process

📋
Phase 01

Voter Registration

Citizens enroll on electoral rolls before a set deadline.

Typically opens 6–12 months before election day. Citizens must be 18+ and provide proof of residence.
Register on NVSP
01
02
🗳️
Phase 02

Candidate Filing

Political parties and independents officially declare candidacy.

Candidates must meet eligibility criteria (age, citizenship) and file nomination papers.
📢
Phase 03

Campaign Period

Candidates actively campaign — rallies, debates, and outreach.

Spending limits apply, and a "silent period" restricts campaigning 48 hours before voting.
03
04
🗳️
Phase 04

Voting Day

Registered voters cast ballots at designated polling booths.

Polls open 7am–6pm. Use ID to vote privately using EVMs.
🔢
Phase 05

Vote Counting

Ballots are counted under strict supervision with party agents present.

EVM counts are tallied constituency-by-constituency. Results are declared round by round.
05
06
🏆
Phase 06

Results Declaration

The Election Commission officially announces winners for each constituency.

The winning party or coalition must demonstrate a majority to form government.
🏛️
Phase 07

Government Formation

The winning leader is invited to form government and takes the oath.

The new Cabinet is sworn in, and the new government's agenda is set.
